Options for YT Bookmark Tagger

Intro

This extension activates when you open a YouTube video.

If the video exists in your browser bookmarks, a bunch of tag buttons will be displayed below the YouTube player.

When one of these are clicked, the #tag will be appended to the bookmark title.

To change the tags available, edit the settings below.

Settings

Tag must not contain whitespace or # characters

Each sub-array is row of tags shown on screen
